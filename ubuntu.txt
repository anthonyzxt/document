#ubuntu常规操作

###apt源报错解决思路
1. 修改DNS  
	<pre>sudo vi /etc/resolvconf/resolv.conf.d/base
	nameserver 8.8.8.8 nameserver 8.8.4.4
	sudo resolvconf -u
	apt-get update</pre>
2. `sudo gedit /etc/apt/source.list`
